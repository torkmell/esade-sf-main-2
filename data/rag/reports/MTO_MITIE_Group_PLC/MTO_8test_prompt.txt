8-TEST FRAMEWORK:

  DIMENSION 1 — SPECIFICITY
  PASS: Named asset/product/division with exact verifiable claim.
  PARTIAL: General direction with some constraint.
  FAIL: Vague language only (e.g. 'committed to sustainability').
  MISSING: No sustainability claim found.

  DIMENSION 2 — METRIC
  PASS: Specific number with unit and context.
  PARTIAL: Direction stated but no quantity.
  FAIL: Number present but misleading or restated after restructuring.
  MISSING: No quantitative data found.

  DIMENSION 3 — BASELINE
  PASS: Explicit baseline year + value, stable methodology.
  PARTIAL: Baseline year stated but no value; or methodology changed.
  FAIL: Baseline cherry-picked or shifted post-acquisition/disposal.
  MISSING: No baseline information found.

  DIMENSION 4 — TARGET
  PASS: SBTi-approved OR legally binding OR board-approved with governance.
  PARTIAL: Company-stated target only, non-binding.
  FAIL: No target; or target contradicts stated behaviour.
  MISSING: No target information found.

  DIMENSION 5 — TIME HORIZON
  PASS: Near-term date ≤2030 with reported interim progress.
  PARTIAL: Long-term date only (>2040); or near-term with no progress.
  FAIL: No date given; or date with no evidence of progress.
  MISSING: No time-related information found.

  DIMENSION 6 — SCOPE
  PASS: Named entities, Scopes 1/2/3, geographies all clearly defined.
  PARTIAL: 'Most operations' without clear definition.
  FAIL: Ambiguous scope likely excluding problem assets.
  MISSING: No scope definition found.

  DIMENSION 7 — VERIFICATION
  PASS: Named verifier + standard (ISAE 3000/3410) + reasonable assurance.
  PARTIAL: Limited assurance only; or subset verification only.
  FAIL: Self-reported only; no external party.
  MISSING: No assurance statement found.

  DIMENSION 8 — CONSISTENCY
  PASS: Capex aligned with claims; no contradictory lobbying; no controversy.
  PARTIAL: Some capex misalignment; minor contradiction.
  FAIL: Capex directly contradicts claim; active contradictory lobbying.
  MISSING: Insufficient disclosure to assess.
